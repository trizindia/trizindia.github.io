Look at your Problem through different lenses The first step to come up with innovative ideas is to look at a problem innovatively i.e look at the problem through different lenses. We tend to look at a problem from only one angle – but how to trigger our mind to look at it from different angles ? Try asking the question what would X do – X could be Steve Jobs or Buddha or Don Quixote or Gandhi or your own Grandfather or Mother-in-law – the idea is to bring a new perspective to the problem.

Resources thinking is the trick of being very smart and only using means that don’t cost anything. The conventional way to solve problems is to throw money at it, but resources thinking is about finding free or very cheap ways to solve problems. The protagonists in “Shawshank Redemption” and “The Count of Monte Cristo” actually use resources to escape out of prison.
