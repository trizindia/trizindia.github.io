How to Be Smarter About the Innovation Trends of the World? Deadline Tomorrow

Can inventing be taught or is it in your genes? The perennial question that hounds a lot of us. G. Altshuller and his colleagues set out to prove that the art of inventing could be taught. So what's up in the world of inventing and invention after they set out with this job? What are some of the hot trends in this field? Here is a great opportunity for you to find out.

GE India is organizing a great one day event to tell you all about the latest and hottest in the inventing world. You can meet TRIZ masters, practitioners and experts who will share their knowledge. One catch - the deadline to register is tomorrow - April 6, 2018.

The brochure is for you to download and spread the word. Please be kind and do that for us :)

More details are given below:

A One Day Summit to Learn, Share and Network

Following the success of TRIZ-TRENDS 2014 and 2016, we are happy to announce that we are back with TRIZ-TRENDS 2018 which is a summit hosted by GE India to explore and share the current practices, applications and evolving trends in the innovation methodology using TRIZ (Russian acronym for Theory of Inventive Problem Solving). This summit will feature Invited talks and a Contributory Poster Session with participation from TRIZ Masters, Experts and Practitioners as well as Innovation thought-leaders from different fields.

The list of speakers, topics and a preliminary agenda will be made available by March 25, 2018.

**Participation & Registration**: Participation is open to Engineers, Scientists, Academicians, Researchers and others interested in practicing and applying TRIZ for Innovation in their respective disciplines on a first-come-first-serve basis limited to a maximum of 50 participants.

Preference will be given to those who have a background in TRIZ through formal or informal learning / training / certification.

REGISTRATION IS FREE BUT MANDATORY.

To Register please send an email to vasant.jain@ge.com with subject as TRIZ-TRENDS 2018 Registration and provide details of Name, Designation, Organization, City, Email ID, Phone and TRIZ relevant certification and experience. Poster Session: Delegates who wish to share their TRIZ related work can do so in the Poster Session. Please indicate your interest in your registration email mentioning the Poster title.

More details will be provided later.

LAST DATE TO REGISTER: April 6, 2018
